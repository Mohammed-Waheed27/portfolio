Project Description

WorkerHive solves the chaos of manual service coordination by creating one connected flow for everyone involved: the customer creates a job, the system assigns the right worker, operations monitors progress, and completion is verified with transparent status updates.

Instead of presenting only visuals, this portfolio version focuses on what matters most to hiring teams: workflow design, architecture decisions, operational reliability, and product impact.
