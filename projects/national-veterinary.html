Project Story

Alwatanyea Veterinary Medicine is a complete private digital ecosystem built from ideation to production to manage veterinary medicine commerce and operations end-to-end.

This is a multi-system project similar in scale to Edu Nasser: one app for user and worker workflows, and another full app for administration and internal business control.

First App (Current Focus)

  • User workflow: browse and order medicines through a structured, reliable mobile journey.
  • Worker workflow: process assigned orders and keep lifecycle states updated accurately.
  • Private onboarding: only approved and authorized company users can use this app.
  • Status visibility: clear request flow from creation to fulfillment.

Second App (Full Internal System)

This second app is a private full e-commerce operations system for company management and governance.

  • Inventory: full stock control, availability tracking, and operations handling.
  • Discounts and pricing: internal discount logic and pricing governance.
  • Users and workers: role, access, and operational responsibility management.
  • Accounting: financial and transaction-related operational visibility.
  • Closed ecosystem: all system modules are private and company-controlled.
